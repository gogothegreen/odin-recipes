Potato massla recipe

Description

Dosa is a popular south Indian pancake-shaped breakfast dish. It is made with a fermented lentils and rice batter. Dosas are usually thin and crispy, and are served with chutney or sambar.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium sized potatoes
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 4 green chillies
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Curry leaves
  • Split bengal gram
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mustard seeds

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes in water thill they are soft but not mushy. Mash them and keep aside.
  2. Chop the onions into long pieces. Chop the chillies into smnall pieces.
  3. Heat 3-4 tablespons of oil in a pot.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and let them cracle.
  5. Then add 1 tablespoon of split bengal gram and fry till golden brown.
  6. Add the curry leaves, onion, and chillies. Fry till onions turn translucent.
  7. Add salt, half a teaspon of turmeric powder and mix well.
  8. Add the potatoes and mix well.
  9. Fill it inside a dosa and enjoy the taste!
