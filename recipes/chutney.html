Coconut chutney recipe

Description

Coconut chutney is a very popular south Indian side which goes along very well with a variety of other dishes.

Ingredients

  • Shredded/dessicated coconut
  • Green chillies
  • Red chillies
  • Ginger
  • Mustard seeds
  • Coconut oil
  • Curry leaves
  • Asaforteida

Steps

  1. Grind 1 cup of coconut along with 1 inch ginger and 3-4 green chillies and set it aside.
  2. In a small utensil, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and let them cracle.
  4. Add 3-4 pinches of asaforteida and salt.
  5. Add red chillies and curry leaves to the oil.
  6. Carefully pour this hot oil on top of the ground coconut paste made earlier.
  7. Serve with dosa or idli.
